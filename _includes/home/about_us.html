About our company

Our interdisciplinary team have backgrounds in conservation, archaeology, and computer science; later specialising in digital applications in the heritage field. We come to heritage first through the lens of our Traditional Skills, giving us a unique perspective and the ability to ask how Digital Heritage can help us to manage, document, conserve, and preserve our Cultural Heritage.

What do we do?

We use traditional skills and innovative techniques to record, preserve, and disseminate cultural heritage.

We provide specialist training to share knowledge with heritage professionals and community groups.

What is our philosophy?

Technology should be at the service of heritage, and not the other way around.

We are committed to delivering the best services in conservation and digital heritage as outlined by internationally recognised standards.

We are passionate about what we do, and we are constantly pushing ourselves to learn new skills and improve the ones we already have.

Empowering local communities to take ownership of their local cultural heritage to ensure its long term preservation

Why collaborate with us?

  • Our blend of skills gives us the ability to implement and create new workflows to fulfil the needs of researchers and professionals in the heritage sector.
  • As conservators, careful attention to detail, ensuring the highest quality work that we can achieve.
  • Our unique perspective gives us the ability to record and query data, finding new solutions.
