About us

Our interdisciplinary team have backgrounds in conservation, archaeology, and computer science; later specialising in digital applications in the heritage field. We come to heritage first through the lens of our Traditional Skills, giving us a unique perspective and the ability to ask how Digital Heritage can help us to preserve, conserve, develop, and manage our Cultural Heritage.

What do we do?

  • We use traditional skills and innovative techniques to record, preserve, and disseminate cultural heritage.
  • We provide specialist training to share knowledge with heritage professionals and community groups.

    • What is our philosophy?

  • Technology should be at the service of heritage, and not the other way around.
  • We are passionate about what we do, therefore, we are constantly pushing ourselves to learn new skills and improve the ones we already have.
  • We are committed to the best practises and international standards that encourage long-term preservation and reusability of data.
  • We believe that communities are key for the long-term preservation of their local heritage.

    • Why collaborate with us?

  • Thanks to our blend of skills we have a unique approach, employing a holistic metholodogy for the conservation and preservation of tangible heritage.
  • As conservators, we pay careful attention to detail, ensuring the highest quality work that we can achieve.
