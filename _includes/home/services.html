Spectrum Heritage is a company workin in conservation and digitial heritage.
We focus on three areas that we believe are fundamental for the future of our cultural heritage.

Conservation

Traditional conservation techniques that respect the original piece, while preserving it for generations to come.

Digital Heritage

Digital heritage augments conservation through the applicaiton of new technology, often opening up new ways to experience or interact with our past.

Training

We strive to be on the cutting edge of digital heritage and conservation, and believe that sharing what we learn along the way helps to enrich all of us as professionals and members of communities alike.
